About this site

An independent job board aggregating open positions across all of The Claremont Colleges.

What is this site?

Claremont College Jobs is a free, independent tool that pulls together open job listings from every institution in The Claremont Colleges consortium into a single searchable page. It is updated daily and is not affiliated with or endorsed by any of the colleges.

Every listing links directly to the official posting on that college's career site, so you apply through the institution's own system — nothing here, no accounts, no data collected.

What the Claremont Colleges are

The Claremont Colleges is a consortium of seven independent institutions located in Claremont, California, about 35 miles east of Los Angeles. Each college has its own faculty, campus, and identity, but they share libraries, dining, transportation, and other services through a central coordinating body — The Claremont Colleges Services (TCCS).

Together they are home to roughly 9,000 students and employ thousands of faculty and staff across academic, administrative, research, and facilities roles.

Why this site saves time

Each college runs its own separate Workday career portal. If you want to see all open positions across the consortium, you would normally have to visit eight different websites, run eight separate searches, and check back on each one individually. This site eliminates that friction.

  • 🔍 One search, all colleges. Filter by job title or school in a single view instead of repeating the same search eight times.
  • 📅 Updated daily. A scheduled script fetches the latest listings from every Workday portal each day, so you always see current openings.
  • 🔔 New listings highlighted. Jobs posted since the last update are marked as new so you can spot fresh opportunities at a glance.
  • ↗️ Apply directly. Every listing links straight to the official posting on the college's own site — no middleman, no account required here.
  • 💻 Open source. The data collection script and this site are publicly available on GitHub if you want to see exactly how it works or contribute.
