Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 5, 2026

This privacy policy describes how claremontcollegejobs.com (the "Site") handles information when you visit. The short version: we do not collect, store, or share any personal information about you.

Who operates this site

This is an independent, non-commercial hobby project. The Site is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to The Claremont Colleges or any of their member institutions.

Information we do not collect

We do not use analytics software, track page views, collect IP addresses, or store any information about visitors on our own servers. There are no user accounts and no sign-up forms.

Local storage

The Site saves three small preferences to your browser's localStorage. This data never leaves your device and is never transmitted to us or any third party.

  • Theme preference — whether you have chosen light or dark mode.
  • Ads preference — whether you have enabled or disabled advertisements.
  • Last refresh time — the timestamp of your most recent data refresh, used to enforce a cooldown between requests.

You can clear this data at any time by clearing your browser's site data for this domain.

Third-party advertising (Google AdSense)

This Site uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google may use cookies and similar technologies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites.

  • You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings.
  • For more information on how Google uses data when you use partner sites, see How Google uses information from sites that use our services.

You can also disable ads entirely using the toggle in the site's About panel (the ? button).

External requests made by the site

When you load or refresh job listings, your browser makes requests to:

  • This site's own files — claremont_jobs_latest.json and claremont_jobs_delta.json, hosted on GitHub Pages. These are standard HTTP requests; no personal data is included.
  • GitHub API — a single request to api.github.com to retrieve the date of the most recent data update, displayed as "last updated." GitHub's own privacy policy applies to this request.

Links to other sites

Job listings link directly to each college's Workday career site. Once you click through, you are subject to that institution's own privacy policy and terms of use.

Children's privacy

This Site is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes materially, the "last updated" date at the top of this page will be revised. Continued use of the Site after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be submitted via the GitHub issues page.
